Concept

This is not just fruit selection.

This is how I imagine FRUTO could communicate.

People don’t always know what fruit to buy, but they often know how they feel.

So instead of asking “What do you want to buy?”, we ask “What do you need today?”

This creates a softer entry point, gives the brand a more memorable tone, and turns fruit into something more personal.

Turn fruit into a response, not just a product.

How I would market FRUTO

Social Content

Turn everyday emotions into content people want to save, repost, and relate to. The fruit becomes a reason to talk, not just a product photo.

Short-form Video

Simple hands-on visuals, clean subtitles, and message-driven rhythm. Low-cost production, but strong enough to build brand tone.

Website Conversion

Reduce decision fatigue through mood-based selection. A more intuitive way to move people from browsing to buying.

KOL / KOC

Work with creators who share daily life naturally. The product feels stronger when it appears in a real routine.

Make FRUTO feel like part of everyday life.
